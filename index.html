Returns & Exchanges

Shop with confidence - 7 days easy returns

1

Initiate Return

Contact us within 7 days of delivery via WhatsApp at +923037990574 or email. Provide order number and reason for return.

2

Pack & Ship

Pack the item securely in original packaging. We'll provide a free return label. Items must be unworn and unwashed with tags intact.

Once received, our team inspects the item. Approved returns are processed within 3-5 business days.
